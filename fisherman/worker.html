About Fisherman Workers

We are a dedicated platform connecting fishing operations with skilled and experienced fisherman workers from the coastal regions of Andhra Pradesh. Our mission is to provide reliable workforce solutions for the fishing industry while supporting local fishing communities.

With deep roots in Andhra's fishing traditions and over 15 years of experience in the fishing industry, we understand the unique challenges and requirements of fishing operations. Our workers are thoroughly vetted, trained in modern techniques, and ready to contribute to your success.

We serve commercial fishing operations, aquaculture facilities, and research expeditions across coastal regions of Andhra Pradesh including Visakhapatnam, Kakinada, Machilipatnam, and more.
